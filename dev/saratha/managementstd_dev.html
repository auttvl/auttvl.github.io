About the Department

Department of Management Studies was established in the year 2008. The department offers two-year full-time MBA programme. Electives are offered in the areas of Operations Management, Financial Management, Human Resource Management and Marketing Management. Excellent academic inputs are being provided by faculty members having expertise and industrial experience. The department strives hard to prepare the students to perform well in the industrial arena. Industry Institute Interaction programmes are also being regularly organized. Executives from the industry interact with the students on a regular basis. Well equipped Labs are provided to improve the research skills and practical exposure of the students. Placement oriented training is provided to all the students with the intention of improving their soft skills. Students are actively involved in organizing seminars, conferences, management meets and industrial visits. The students also enrich their business knowledge through business quiz, case study and group discussion.
