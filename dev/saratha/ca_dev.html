About the Department

    The Department of Computer Applications is offering MCA degree programme from the year 2008. The department is persistent in providing an excellent pathway to widen and enrich the knowledge horizons of the student community. The self-motivated and hardworking faculty members of the department ensure that the student learn strong values and develop fully integrated personality to face the future with courage and confidence. The students are encouraged to improve their knowledge in major areas of research such as Grid Computing, Cloud Computing, Data Structures and Algorithms, Image Processing, Data Mining, Neural Networks, Deep Learning, Fuzzy Logic and so on. The department focuses on quality learning environment for students through dedicated and experienced faculty members. The department organizes seminars, workshop, symposium and training programmes regularly to cater to the knowledge thirst of faculty members and the students. A well-equipped laboratory with adequate number of systems is available for the students to learn the paradigm of programming with applicable knowledge.
